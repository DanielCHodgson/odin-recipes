Beans on Toast Recipe

Decription

This is how you make beans on toast. A perfect filler for your belly and for this website.

Ingredients

  • Tin of beans. Preferably Branston.
  • Thick cut white bread. Preferably the synthetic Warburtons toastie type.
  • Butter.

Steps

  1. Add the beans to a saucepan and heat gently, stirring so the beans don't stick to the bottom.
  2. Meanwhile, add the bread to a toaster and toast until desired doneness. At least to a deep golden colour all over (anaemic toast enjoyers deserve the gulag).
  3. Once the toast is done, butter it and slice diagonally. Diagonally slicing bread significantly improves the flavour.
  4. Once the beans are piping hot, add to the buttered toast.
