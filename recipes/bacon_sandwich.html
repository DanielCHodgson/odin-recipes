Bacon Sandwich Recipe

Decription

This is how you make a perfect bacon sandwhich. Period. Fact. Undesputable. And before you start, I don't wanna hear it. Just follow the damn recipe.

Ingredients

  • 3 slices of thick cut smoked back bacon OR 5 slices of thin cut smoked back bacon.
  • Thick cut fresh bread of choice. A crusty cob is king.
  • Butter.
  • Ketchup and / or brown sauce. I won't judge.

Steps

  1. Line a baking sheet with tinfoil (the good kind, the cheap stuff is a false economy), then lay out the bacon. Make sure to leave a little room between slices so the bacon has maximum surface area with the baking sheet.
  2. Add the baking sheet to a cold oven (this will give the fat time to render) and set to 200 fan.
  3. Cook for 15-20 minutes depending on your bacon thickness. It may need even longer depending on how crispy you like it.
  4. If needed, drain any excess liquid from the baking sheet halfway through cooking. The lower quality of bacon the higher chance it'll give out a load of brine which will stop your bacon crisping.
  5. While the bacon is cooking, butter the bread and add your condiments.
  6. Once the bacon has reached your desired crisp level (it better have crisp, anaemic bacon enjoyers deserve the gulag), remove from the oven.
  7. Assemble the sandwich and slice diagonally (this significantly improves the taste). However, a vertical slice is recommended if this would result in a more geomertically balanced sandwich than a diagonal.
