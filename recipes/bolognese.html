Bolognese Recipe

Decription

This lasagna layers rich meat sauce and creamy béchamel between tender pasta sheets. Start with a savory blend of ground beef and Italian pancetta cooked with onions, garlic, and chopped tomatoes.

Layer the meat sauce, béchamel, and lasagna noodles in a baking dish, repeating until full. Top with extra béchamel, mozerella and Parmesan and bake until bubbly and golden. Let it rest before serving for perfect slices.

Ingredients

For the ragu

  • 650g ground beef
  • 100g diced pancetta
  • 1 white onion
  • 1-2 medium carrots
  • 2-3 celery stalks
  • tomato puree
  • 1 large glass red wine
  • 350ml beef stock
  • 2 400g cans of tomatoes
  • 1 handful basil leaves
  • 200ml whole milk
  • lasagna sheets

For the white sauce

  • 100g butter
  • 100g plain flour
  • 500m whole milk
  • pinch of ground nutmeg

Steps

Make the ragu

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce: Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add diced pancetta, cooking until crispy. Add carrots, onion, and celery, and sauté until softened.
  2. the Beef: Add the ground beef to the pan, breaking it apart and cooking until browned.
  3. Add Tomato and Wine: Stir in tomato puree, cooking for 2 minutes. Pour in red wine and let it reduce by half.
  4. Simmer: Add beef stock, canned tomatoes, and chopped basil. Simmer for 30-40 minutes. Gradually stir in milk to add creaminess. Season with salt and pepper.

Make the white sauce

  1. melt the butter in a saucepan, then stir in the flour for a couple of minutes to cook off the raw taste.
  2. Slowly add the milk and constantly whisk on medium heat until the sauce consistency coats the back of a spoon.
  3. Add the nutmeg and season with salt and pepper.

Assemble and bake

  1. Layer the lasagna in a baking dish starting with ragu, then lasagna sheets, then white sauce, and repeat.
  2. Top the final layer of white sauce with mozeralla and parmasan.
  3. Bake in the oven at 200 for about 30 minutes. If the top layer starts to overcook, cover the lasagna with tinfoil.
  4. Let rest for at least 15 minutes before slicing and serving.
